Simple Omelette Recipe

an easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal .this classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats

Preparation Time

  • Total:Approxmately 10 minutes
  • Preparation:5 minutes
  • Cooking:5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 Large eggs
  • Salt, to Taste
  • Pepper, to Tast
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings : cheese , diced vegetables , cooked meats , herbs

Instructions

  • Beat the eggs:in a bowl , beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they well mixed . you can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture.
  • Heat the pan place non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil
  • Cook the omeletteonce is better melted and bubbling pour in the egg tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat there surface
  • Add feelings (optional)when the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still sightly Runny in the middle a sprinkle your chosen feeling over one half of the omelette
  • Fold and serviceas the omelet continue to cook carefully lift one Edge and fold it over the feelings let it cook for another minute then slide it unto a plat
  • Enjoy serve hot with additional Salt and Pepper if needed
